## 1. Program Name

This messaging program is called **LocalDink Alerts**.

## 2. What Messages You Receive

Messages are transactional and service-related, including game invites, RSVP updates, reminders, and session changes.

Examples might include something like: "LocalDink: You are invited to a pickleball game at Central Park Courts tomorrow at 5:00 PM. Reply YES to join or NO to decline. Reply STOP to opt out." or "LocalDink: Reminder - your game starts in 2 hours at Central Park Courts. Reply CANCEL if you cannot make it. Reply STOP to opt out."

## 3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies, typically 2–10 messages per week depending on your game activity.

## 4. Opt-In Flow (Web-Visible Verification)

1. User enters phone number during LocalDink account setup.
2. User verifies phone ownership with a one-time SMS code.
3. User enables SMS notifications in app settings.

Users can disable SMS notifications at any time in LocalDink settings. 

At the point of opt-in, users see: "By enabling SMS notifications, you agree to receive text messages from LocalDink Alerts about games, RSVPs, and reminders. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help. See our Privacy Policy and Terms."
